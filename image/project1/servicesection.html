Our Services

At LifeCare Health Center we specialize in General Medicine
General medicine is the comprehensive primary care services that address everyday health concerns, routine checkups, and management of chronic conditions.
At LifeCare Health Center we also perform Surgical Services
Our modern surgical unit providing safe and effective operations, ranging from minor procedures to advanced surgeries.
Pathology & Laboratory Testing
We provide diagnostic services including blood work, microbiology, immunology, and other lab tests to ensure accurate health assessments.
Radiology & Imaging
We perform advanced imaging technologies such as X-rays, ultrasounds, and scans to support precise diagnosis and treatment planning.
Maternal & Child Healthcare
Specialized care for expectant mothers, newborns, and children, focusing on safe deliveries, vaccinations, and pediatric wellness.
Emergency & Trauma Care
Our 24/7 emergency services designed to provide immediate medical attention for accidents, injuries, and urgent health crises.
