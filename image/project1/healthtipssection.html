STAY HYDRATED

Water is life it fuels your body, sharpens your mind and keep you running.

  1. Start your morning with a glass of water.
  2. A glass of water before meal aids digestion and keeps you on track.
  3. Eat water rich foods like fruits and vegetables.

Move Regularly

Moving regularly improves blood circulation, boost energy, sharpen focus and protect long term health.

  1. Take the stairs skip the elevator.
  2. Pace during phone calls, walk while talking.
  3. Standup every 30-60 minutes stretch or walk around for couple of minutes.

Protect Your Mental Health

Protecting mental health isn't about avoiding challenges, it's about building resilience, nurting positive habits and having joy and calm in everyday habit.

  1. Connect with loved ones.
  2. Engage in activities that brings you joy.
  3. Take break when needed.
