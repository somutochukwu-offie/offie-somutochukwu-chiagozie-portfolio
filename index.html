About Me

My name is Offie Somutochukwu Chiagozie, and I am from Aku, in Igbo-etiti L.G.A Enugu State, Nigeria. I am currently a student learning full-stack web development at Turing Tech. I enjoy learning, exploring new ideas, and connecting with people in meaningful ways. I value creativity, knowledge, and strong relationships. I enjoy exploring new ideas, analyzing challenges, and developing practical solutions that create impact.

I am the fourth child of a family of seven children, five boys and two girls. My dad is a retired civil servant, and my mum a teacher. As a child, i enjoyed reading and learning, which helped me in school, I wasn't the brightest in class but i didn't perform badly in my subjects as well. Skipping some of my elementary classes helped me graduate elemantary school at a very young age and also aided graduating secondary school before clocking eighteen years.

My family is my foundation and motivation, where i learned most of the good i know, honesty, hardwork and commitment to purpose etc. I owe my parents a lot for providing enabling environment for me thus I can't repay all they have sacrificed for me. I pray to be bouyant enough to be able to provide for them, while they are still alive.

I am learning web development, because we are in a tech world, being tech savy with good knowledge of full-stack, A.I, python etc. will open many doors to many industries/businesses. Currently, Nigeria or Africa at large, is a very fertile market for technical innovations, with very young and entrepreneurial population and unique infrastructural challenges that drives creative solutions etc. Tech is the now and the future, so i embraced it.
